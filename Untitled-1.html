Early experiences

Born in Hangzhou City, Zhejiang Province, on September 10, 1964, Ma Yun's grandfather had done during the War of Resistance against Japanese Aggression, after the liberation was classified as "Black Five", named "Ma Yun", is the hope that Ma Yun clever sensible, less trouble.At the age of 12, Ma Yun bought a pocket radio, from then on every day to listen to English radio, began to be interested in English, 13 years old, Ma Yun because of fighting too much demerit, had been forced to transfer to Hangzhou No. 8 Middle School.Ma then took the high school entrance exam, which took him two years before he was admitted to a very ordinary high school, scoring only 31 points in math once.

In 1983, Ma took the gaokao for the second time, failed again, and improved his math score to 19 points.After his parents persuaded him to put off going to college and learn a trade, Mr. Ma began riding the old bicycle around Hangzhou. In 1984, Ma Yun took the college entrance examination for the third time despite the strong opposition of his family. He got 89 points in the math test, but the total separation of undergraduate line was still 5 points short.Due to the enrollment quota of English major is not full, some excellent English students get the opportunity to upgrade this year, Ma Yun was promoted to the undergraduate major of foreign language by Hangzhou Normal University.After entering the university, Ma Yun became a good student who excelled both in character and study, and sat steadily in the top five of the foreign language department with his excellent English.Ma was later elected president of the student union and later served two terms as president of the Hangzhou Students' Federation.
